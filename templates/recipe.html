Spicy Chicken Curry

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

A rich and flavorful Indian chicken curry, perfect with rice or naan. This recipe uses fresh spices and tender chicken pieces for an authentic taste.

Prep: 20 min Cook: 45 min Servings: 4 Calories: 450

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for 30 seconds, stirring constantly.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, until the oil separates from the mixture (about 5-7 minutes).
  5. Add chicken pieces and salt. Cook until chicken is lightly browned on all sides.
  6. Reduce heat to low. Stir in the plain yogurt gradually, ensuring it doesn't curdle.
  7. Add 1/2 cup of water (or more for a thinner gravy). Bring to a simmer, then cover and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and tender.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Nutrition Information (Approximate per serving):

  • Calories: 450 kcal
  • Protein: 40g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Fat: 20g
